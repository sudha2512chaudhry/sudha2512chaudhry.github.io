Publication


A Survey on Long Wide Area Network (LoRaWAN)

(Dec 2019)

Under the supervision of Prof. Ipseeta Nanda, my research article "A Survey on Long Wide Area Network (LoRaWAN)" was published in Volume 2, Issue 2 - 2019, A Journal of Advanced Research in Networking and Communication Engineering. The research article is about LoRaWAN (Long Range Wide Area Network) technology and about its architecture and various applications.
